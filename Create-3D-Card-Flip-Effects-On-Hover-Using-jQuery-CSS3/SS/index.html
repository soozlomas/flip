Participate. Mentor. Share a challenge.

Led by GHD, Smart Seeds runs for 10 weeks each year, bringing together bright young minds from different disciplines and organisations to work on innovative concepts for our infrastructure industry.

Challenge authors put forward their challenges in the infrastructure sector at the Challenge Workshop.

Participants form multidisciplinary teams from different organisations to generate ideas to solve the challenges.

At the end of 10 weeks, teams pitch their ideas to a judging panel of senior industry leaders at the exclusive Showcase Event.
